Traffic Light Controller

The traffic light controller in VHDL is used for an intersection between different road vehicles. The traffic light for all other ways except the 1st way turns to YELLOW, then RED so that the cars from the 1st way can cross the highway. And vice versa for all the respective ways for 60 seconds. The time period is 3 seconds for the YELLOW light and 10 seconds for the RED light.

Arduino-Based Musical Fountain

The basic idea of this Arduino Water Fountain is to take an input from any external sound source like mobile, iPod, PC etc., sample the sound and break it down to different voltage ranges, then use the output to turn on various Relay.

The higher voltage range will correspond to a relay switch ON which comprises a musical water fountain operating to the beats and rhythms of the song. So here we are building this Musical Fountain using Arduino and sound sensor.
